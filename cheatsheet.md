Commands~

Basic Commands:
    look / look around – Observe the current environment
    inspect <object> – Examine a specific object
    notice <object> – Pay closer attention to something
    talk <person> – Speak to a character (if available)
    open <object> – Open an item (if possible)
    take <object> – Pick up an item
    drop <object> – Remove an item from your inventory
    place <object> – Set an item down intentionally
    use <object> – Use an item (if applicable)
    go – Move forward or progress to the next area
    enter – Attempt to enter a location
Support Commands:
    help – Displays available actions in the current scene
    inventory – Shows items currently held
    journal – Displays journal options
    read journal – View notes
    write journal – Add a note
Scene-Specific Commands:
    cab / walk – Choose how to travel in the Intro
    take suitcase / leave suitcase – Decide what to do with the suitcase
    talk lestrade – Speak with Lestrade in the House
    pickpocket donovan – Attempt a risky action
    leave – Exit the house (with options)
    inspect suitcase – Interact with the locked suitcase
    (type the code when prompted) – Unlock the suitcase
    tell watson – Share information
    hide suitcase – Conceal information
    give to police – Hand over evidence
    think – Reflect on clues
    confront – Face the cabbie in the final scene


Specific Challenges:
    Identify key clues at the crime scene (at least 3 required to leave safely)
    Remember the suitcase code (RACHE)
    Decide whether to trust Watson or hide information
    Avoid giving evidence to the police too early
    Correct choices lead to the best ending; poor decisions can result in failure or death

Endings~

Best Ending (Win):
    Solve the suitcase (open it and the phone)
    Tell Watson about what you found
    Do NOT give the suitcase to the police
    Outcome: You and Watson are prepared. The cabbie is stopped, and the case is successfully solved.
    

Incomplete Ending:
    Reach the final confrontation
    But miss key steps (e.g., incomplete investigation or unclear decisions)
    Outcome:You survive, but the case is not fully resolved.

Fail Ending (Police):
    Give the suitcase to the police
    Outcome: You lose control of the case. The cabbie escapes, and the investigation fails.
    
Death Ending:
    Hide the truth from Watson (do NOT tell him)
    Proceed to the final confrontation alone
    Outcome: You face the cabbie unprepared and die. The case ends with you.

How to win!
    Investigate thoroughly in the house
    Keep the suitcase and solve it
    Follow the clue to the cabbie
    Trust Watson and share information
    Maintain control of the evidence
